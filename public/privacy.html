1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services, or otherwise when you contact us.

The personal information we collect may include: email addresses (if you subscribe to our newsletter).

Sensitive Information. We do not process sensitive information.

Important note about your financial data: All financial information you enter into the Vestly calculator is processed entirely within your browser. This data is never transmitted to our servers, never stored, and never visible to anyone — including Vestly.

Information automatically collected

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our Services, such as your IP address, browser type, device characteristics, and usage information. This information is collected through Vercel Analytics and does not reveal your specific identity.

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. Your financial data entered into the calculator never leaves your browser and is never stored on any server.

We do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18 years of age. By using our Services, you represent that you are at least 18 years old.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@getvestly.ca.
